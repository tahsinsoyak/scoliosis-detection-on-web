About Cobb Angle Detection

This application detects the Cobb angle from X-ray images to help in diagnosing scoliosis. The Cobb angle is a measure of the curvature of the spine in degrees, which is critical for determining the severity of scoliosis.

Our system uses advanced machine learning models to accurately detect vertebrae in X-ray images, calculate their middle points, and determine the Cobb angle.
